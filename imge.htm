Getting to Woxsen

The lush green Woxsen University 200 acre, eco-friendly campus is located at Sadashivpet, about 60 kms (37 miles) from the main city of Hyderabad. Students and visitors can easily avail taxi service from the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport and Nampally Railway Station to campus. We also have a regular shuttle service will be operating between the campus and Hyderabad City, which students can use to commute outside the campus.
